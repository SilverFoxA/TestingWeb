    TeamKrishna

    Redifining education with the promise of freedom.


    Team Krishna is the first team to register for the Global Learning XPRIZE Challenge. We are a group of life long learners who believe that every child must have access to quality learning.
    Our vision is to create a child-centric model of learning by means of an elaborate gaming environment underpinned by a robust, relevant curriculum. This will allow destitute kids to self-learn in environments that are not penetrated by formal education, and support those kids who are not fortunate enough to get good schooling environments.

    Organized as a volunteer’s team, we are supported by:
    Our engineers: who are excited about the challenge of programming machine learning such that a child is directed to learning environments based on child's performance. Our software dares to create an autonomous learning environment for children, where they are engaged in a game which enables them to learn to read, write and speak

    Our research team: who are having a brilliant time creating a curriculum whose structure is flexible enough for children to engage and grow from diverse starting points.
